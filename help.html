❓ Help & Support

Find answers to common questions

What is Indispeak?
Indispeak is an interactive language learning platform designed to help you learn Indian languages including Hindi, Bengali, Marathi, Gujarati, and many more. Learn at your own pace with interactive lessons and quizzes.
How do I complete a lesson?
To complete a lesson, navigate to your desired language course, select a lesson, read through the vocabulary and pronunciation guide, then click "Mark as Completed" at the bottom of the lesson page.
Can I track my progress?
Yes! Your profile page shows your total lessons completed, quiz attempts, and average score. You can also see which lessons you've completed in the "My Courses" section.
How do quizzes work?
Quizzes test your knowledge of languages. Answer multiple-choice questions, and your score will be saved to your profile. You can retake quizzes anytime to improve your score.
How do I change my profile picture?
Go to your Profile page, click the "Edit Profile" tab, select a new profile picture from your device, and click "Save Changes".
How can I reset my password?
Go to your Profile page, click the "Change Password" tab, enter your current password and new password, then click "Update Password".
What languages can I learn?
Indispeak offers courses in: Marathi, Hindi, Malayalam, Gujarati, Assamese, Telugu, Odia, Kannada, Konkani, Rajasthani, Manipuri, and Bengali.
